## Phone Recording Tips

- **Landscape mode** — hold phone sideways to match the game's aspect ratio
- **Stabilize** — lean phone against something or use both hands
- **Reduce glare** — dim room lights, increase screen brightness
- **No shaky zoom** — keep a steady distance, fill the frame with the screen
- **Audio** — keep the game audio on so the chiptune music and SFX are captured
- **Multiple takes** — record 3-4 runs and pick the best moments
